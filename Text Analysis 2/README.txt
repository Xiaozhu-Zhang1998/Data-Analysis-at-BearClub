Text Analysis 2 - Langya Bang (《琅琊榜》)

Files Description:
1. "琅琊榜.txt" is the novel/ text to be analyzed.
2. "langya_words_sogou.txt" and "langya.scel" are libraries of special words in this novel.
3. "stopwords.dat" is the library of stop words.
4. "tags.csv" is the list of parts of speech.
5. "主角名单.txt" is the list of characters in the novel.
6. "主要地点.txt" is the main locations in the novel.
